Shichao Wu 吴仕超

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in Artificial Intelligence at Nankai University, advised by Prof. Jingtai Liu.

I received my B.E. in Automation from Southwest University of Science and Technology, Mianyang, China, in 2017, and my M.S. in Robot Science and Engineering from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China, in 2020.

My research interests include context-aware emotion recognition, sound events localization and localization (SELD), sound-enabled service robots, and embodied artificial intelligence.
