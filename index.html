Shichao Wu 吴仕超

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in Artificial Intelligence at Nankai University, advised by Prof. Jingtai Liu.

I received my B.E. in Automation from Southwest University of Science and Technology, Mianyang, China, in 2017, and my M.S. in Robot Science and Engineering from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China, in 2020.

My research interests include context-aware emotion recognition, sound events localization and localization (SELD), sound-enabled service robots, and embodied artificial intelligence.

Previous publications (works before 2020)

Before I start my journey at NKU, I studied at SWUST with my partners concerning robotics under the adviser of A.P. Manlu Liu and Prof. Hua Zhang from 06/2014 to 06/2017 in Robot Technology Used for Special Environment Key Laboratory of Sichuan Province.

After that, I spent two and a half years working with Prof. Fei Wang on EEG-based fatigue detection & emotion recognition, and brain-computer interface from 09/2017 to 01/2020 in the human-robot cooperation laboratory. Representative papers are highlighted.
